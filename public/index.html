PixelMind AI.


PixelMind AI is a 24-hour long hackathon, and will provide an exciting opportunity for all IIT Madras BS Students to showcase their skills in AI/ML.

This event will be open to all IIT Madras BS students & participants will either participate individually or work in teams of 2-5 members to develop their solutions to a challenging problem statement related to image processing.


The participants will also compete for exciting prizes & also an opportunity to intern with Fotografik Enterprises Pvt. Ltd.

This hackathon will offer a unique opportunity for students to showcase their skills and interact with like-minded peers and professionals in the field. There will also be various goodies provided by Fotografik for all participants!



Unleash your AI/ML skills in Image Processing: Compete, Innovate and Win!
