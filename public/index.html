HACK IN THE WEST

Welcome to Hack in the West.
A 30-hour hackathon where programmers, designers and inventors meet to code, design and build innovative and promising solutions to real world problems.
This is a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology.


It doesn't matter if someone is still making their first app or their 100th one. There are a lot of things to learn. This is an opportunity for programmers to boost their technical skills and also their ability to think laterally.
The only contraint is the time!
